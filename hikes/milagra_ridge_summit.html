Route
From Milagra Ridge parking lot, walk along Summit Trail to reach the summit. Return by the same route or along a paved bike trail. There are several vista points along the trail that offer panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, the city of Pacifica and Sharp Park Beach.
Trailhead

Location: Intersection of Sharp Park Road & College Drive, Pacifica, CA 94044 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From 280 North, take exit 41 to merge onto CA-35 North towards Skyline Blvd / Pacifica. After 3.5 miles, turn left onto Sharp Park Blvd. In 0.6 Miles, Milagra Ridge is at the intersection with College Drive.
