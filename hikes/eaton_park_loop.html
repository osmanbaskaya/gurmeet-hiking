Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map by City of San Carlos: Good map showing various trails. Elevation profile and trail segment lengths are missing.

» Redwoodhikes Trail Map: Great map showing many parks like Purisima Creek, Huddart, Wunderlich, El Corte De Madera, Foothills, Hidden Villa and Rancho San Antonio. Trail maps for individual parks are available online for free. However, the RedwoodHikes map shows the big picture and is helpful for planning hikes that go through multiple parks.

Route
This hike stays within Eaton Park and covers all major trails. There are no signs at trail junctions. It is best to study the Official Trail Map (some trails are missing in this map) and the GPS Tracks (see link at the top of the page) to infer the route. Trail Notes by City of San Carlos Official Website may be helpful for this hike.

Trailhead

Location: near 2998 Eaton Avenue, Redwood City, CA . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From I-280 North, take exit 29 for Edgewood Rd, turn right onto Edgewood Rd and drive for 2.1 miles. Turn left onto Scenic Rd and drive 0.2 miles. Turn right onto Clifford Ave and drive 0.2 miles. Turn left onto Eaton Ave and drive 0.4 miles to reach a cul-de-sac with an entrance for Eaton Park.
