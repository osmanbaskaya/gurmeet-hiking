Route

To Rancho Canada Del Oro Parking Lot: From the parking lot along McKean Road, follow Figueroa Trail (0.2 miles) → right on Los Cerritos Trail (2.5 miles) → right on Pena Trail (0.4 miles) → Javelina Loop (1.1 miles) → Cottle Trail (1.3 miles) → Chisnantuck Peak Trail (1.0 miles) → right on Bald Peaks Trail (0.2 miles) → left on Catamount Trail (1.7 miles) → right on Longwall Canyon Trail (0.5 miles) → Mayfair Ranch Trail (3.1 miles) to Rancho Canada Del Oro Parking Lot.

To Calero Parking Lot: Follow an unnamed trail (0.1 miles) → right on Serpentine Loop Trail (1.4 miles) → right on Canada del Oro Cutoff (0.2 miles) → Canada Del Oro Trail (0.8 miles) → right on Figueroa Trail (1.4 miles) to reach Calero Parking Lot.

Trailhead

Location: on McKean Road in San Jose, CA 95037 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 101, take the Bailey Road exit and head west 2.7 miles to McKean Road. Turn left and follow the signs to the trail head for Figueora Trail (see trail map).
