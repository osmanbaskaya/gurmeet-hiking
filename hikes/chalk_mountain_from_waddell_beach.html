Chalk Mountain from Waddell Beach

Summary

A steep hike through rugged terrain to a nice vista point with views of the Pacific and Santa Cruz mountains.

Location: Big Basin Redwoods State Park

Route

To Chalk Mountain (6.8 miles): From Waddell Beach, follow Skyline to the Sea Trail (paved road for 0.4 miles) → left on Skyline to the Sea Trail (1.3 miles) → left on Clark Connection Trail (1.0 miles) → Westridge Trail (4.0 miles) → right on Chalks Road to an overlook on Chalk Mountain (0.1 miles).

To Waddell Beach (6.8 miles): Retrace your steps back to the beach.

Notes:

  1. At the intersection of Clark Connection Trail and Skyline to the Sea Trail, there is a sign saying that Westridge Trail is 2.0 miles and Chalk Mountain is 9.0 miles. These are probably estimates for the round trip. The actual one-way mileages are 1.0 miles and 5.1 miles respectively.
  2. Many steep sections along the way. See elevation profile.
  3. Very few people visit Chalk Mountain due to the ruggedness of terrain. On a Sunday morning in April 2013, we did not meet anybody else.
