Route
Follow Kennedy Trail (3.8 miles) to reach an intersection with Priest Rock Trail. Continue along Kennedy Trail (1.4 miles) to reach an intersection with Limekiln Trail. Turn right on Limekiln Trail (2.9 miles) → right on Priest Rock Trail (1.4 miles) → left on Kennedy Trail (3.8 miles) to return to trailhead.
