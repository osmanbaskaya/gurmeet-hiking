Route
New Almaden Trail (0.2 + 1.0 + 1.3 miles) → Prospect #3 Trail (0.3 miles, steep) → Randol Trail (1.3 + 0.4 + 0.3 + 0.5 + 0.5 miles) → Capehorn Pass Trail (0.3 miles) → Hacienda Trail (0.3 miles) → Virl O Norton Trail (1.2 miles).

Notes: Mine Trail and Randol Trail are wide unpaved roads. New Almaden Trail is a narrow hiking trail.

Trailhead

Location: within 0.1 miles of 19305 Mockingbird Hill Lane, San Jose, CA 95120 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 85, take Almaden Blvd south. Drive for 4.2 miles, turn right on Almaden Rd, drive 0.6 miles. Turn right on Mockingbird Hill Ln and drive less than half a mile to reach a large parking lot.
