Trailhead

Location: along Patterson Ranch Road, Fremont, CA 94555 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: Along CA-84, take exit 36 for Thornton Ave towards Paseo Padre Pkwy. Turn left onto Thornton Ave, then continue along Paseo Padre Parkway. In one mile, turn left onto Patterson Ranch Road. Within 1.4 miles, Coyote Hills Visitor Cener is on your left.

Parking Fees: $5 per vehicle (last visited: March 2010)

Capacity: About 20 cars
