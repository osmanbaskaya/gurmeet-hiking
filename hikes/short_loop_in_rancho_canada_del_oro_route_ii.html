Route
From the parking lot, follow an unnamed short trail leading to Calero County Park trails → left on Serpentine Loop Trail (0.5 miles) → Longwall Canyon Trail (0.2 miles) → right on Catamount Trail (1.7 miles) → left on Bald Peaks Trail (1.0 miles) → Longwall Canyon Trail (2.7 miles) → Serpentine Loop Trail (0.5 miles) → unnamed short trail to parking lot.
Trailhead

Location: 4289 Casa Loma Rd Morgan Hill, CA 95037 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: (PDF) Map for Directions. From Highway 101 South, take the Bailey Avenue exit west. Then turn left onto McKean Road. Turn right onto Casa Loma Road. The staging area is at the end of the paved road on your left.
