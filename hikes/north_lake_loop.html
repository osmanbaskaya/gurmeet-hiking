Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Good map showing various trails. Mileage information is missing.

» Walker's Map of San Francisco by Pease Press: Excellent quality maps showing many parks and trails in the San Francisco area. Many of these parks do not have free trail maps online. Cost was $7.95 in April 2011.

Route

Trailhead: Parking lot on Harding Road, near Hwy 35 (Skyline Blvd).

Route: Follow the paved trail around North Lake (marked by "1" in the trail map). The western section of the trail adjoins the busy Skyline Blvd. The northern section of the trail adjoins Lake Merced Blvd which has much less traffic. The eastern section goes through Harding Park Golf Course (marked by "5" in the trail map).

Trailhead

Location: near intersection of Harding Road and Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd) in San Francisco, CA 94132 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Skyline Blvd (CA-35) North in San Francisco, turn right onto Harding Road. Drive for 0.1 miles to reach a large parking lot.
