Route

To Castle Rock Park HQ (6.9 miles): From Saratoga Gap parking lot at the intersection of Hwy 9 and Hwy 35, follow Skyline Trail (1.7 miles). Cross over Hwy 35 and walk along Loghry Woods Trail (1.8 miles) → right on Service Road Trail (0.1 miles) → left on Saratoga Gap Trail (2.5 miles) → right on Castle Rock Trail (0.8 miles) to reach Park HQ.

To Saratoga Gap parking lot (3.4 miles): From Castle Rock Park HQ, cross Hwy 35. Visit Indian Rock (0.3 miles) and return to Hwy 35. Turn right and walk all along Skyline Trail (3.1 miles) to return to Saratoga Gap trailhead.
