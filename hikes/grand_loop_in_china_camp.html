Grand Loop in China Camp

Summary

Great long hike through varied terrain including shoreline walks and green rolling hills.

Location: China Camp State Park

Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Good map showing various trails.

Route

Trailhead: Parking lot at China Camp Point.

Route in brief: Shoreline Trail → Peacock Gap Trail → Oak Ridge Trail → Bay View Trail → Shoreline Trail. Take a short detour to encircle Turtle Back Hill (0.65 miles). You may also spend some time at a small beach at Bullhead Flat.

Trailhead

Location: along N Sen Pedro Road, San Rafael, CA 94901 (0.7 miles north of its intersection with Biscayne Drive) . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From US-101 North, take exit 452 toward Central San Rafael, then turn right onto 2nd St and drive 0.3 miles. Continue onto 3rd St and drive 0.4 miles. Turn slightly left onto Point San Pedro Road and drive for 4.8 miles. The parking lot for China Camp State Park is on the right.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: $5 per vehicle (last visited: September 2010)

Capacity: Over 25 cars may be parked here.
