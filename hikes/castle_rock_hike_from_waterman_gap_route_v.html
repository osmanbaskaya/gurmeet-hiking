Castle Rock Hike from Waterman Gap - Route V

Summary

Pleasant hike in the western section of Castle Rock.

Location: Castle Rock State Park — Saratoga Gap Open Space Preserve

Route

To Beekhuis Road Trail (5.2 miles): From the intersection of Hwy 236 and Hwy 9, find signposts for Skyline to the Sea Trail going south towards Big Basin Park HQ. Follow Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.6 miles) → left on a connector trail that goes to Saratoga Toll Road (1.5 miles). The connector trail crosses Hwy 9. Upon reaching Hwy 9, turn left and walk a few steps to see a signpost marking the connector trail. Continue along the Connector Trail (0.2 miles), then turn left on Saratoga Toll Road (2.9 miles) to reach an intersection with Beekhuis Road Trail.

To Saratoga Gap (3.3 miles): Continue along Saratoga Toll Road (0.8 miles) to reach a junction with Travertine Springs Trail. Continue along Saratoga Toll Road (2.0 miles) to reach an intersection with Skyline to the Sea Trail. Continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.5 miles) to return to trail head.

Saratoga Gap / Long Ridge (3.8 miles): From the trail head, follow Saratoga Gap Trail (1.7 miles) → Bay Area Ridge Trail (0.3 miles) to reach Highway 35. Cross over the highway. Then enter Long Ridge Open Space Preserve. Follow Bay Area Ridge Trail (0.1 miles) → Achistaca Trail (1.7 miles).

To Waterman Gap at Intersection of Hwy 9 and Hwy 236 (6.0 miles): Turn right on Skyline to the Sea Trail going south (1.5 miles). Cross Highway 9 again to continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (1.5 miles). At this point, there is a junction with Beekhuis Road Trail. In Nov 2012, this junction was unmarked. Continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (3.0 miles) to reach the intersection of Hwy 9 and Hwy 236.

Notes: Along Saratoga Toll Road, near the intersection with Beekhuis Road Trail, there were some unmarked intersections in Dec 2012. We followed the strategy of avoided trails which were losing elevation quickly, and didn't get lost. There were unmarked intersections along Saratoga Toll Road close to the trailhead as well.
