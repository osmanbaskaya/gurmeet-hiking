Route
Follow Woods Trail (2.8 miles) to reach an intersection with Barlow Road. Continue along Woods Trial (3.6 miles) → left on Limekiln Trail (2.9 miles) → right on Preist Rock Trail (1.4 miles) → right on Kennedy Trail (1.4 miles) → continue along Woods Trail (3.6 + 2.8 miles) to return to trailhead.
