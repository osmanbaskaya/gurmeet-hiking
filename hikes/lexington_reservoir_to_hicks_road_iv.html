Route
Walk along Alma Road going north (0.4 miles) → right on Limekiln Trail (2.2 miles) to reach intersection with Priest Rock Trail. Continue along Limekiln Trail (2.9 miles) → right on Woods Trail (3.6 miles) to reach intersection with Barlow Road. Continue along Woods Trail (2.8 miles) to reach Hicks Road.
