Monument Peak from Ed Levin
9.2 miles     2200 ft

Summary

Great hike through green rolling hills with panoramic views of San Francisco Bay.

Location: Ed Levin County Park

Route

Trailhead: Start at Sandy Wool Group Area.

Ascent (4.0 miles): Tularcitos Trail (0.3 miles) → Agua Caliente Trail (0.4 + 1.0 miles) → Monument Peak Trail (1.4 + 0.3 + 0.6 miles).

Descent (4.8 miles): Monument Peak Road (0.25 + 0.15 + 0.6 + 1.5 + 0.1 miles) → Agua Caliente Trail (0.2 miles) → Calera Creek Trail (1.5 miles) → paved road (0.2 + 0.3 miles)

Notes:

  1. Hang gliders may be seen in air during the hike. Locations of hang gliding take off points and landing points are marked in the official trail map.
  2. The section of Service Road that is adjacent to 'Private Residences' (see Trail Map) is not open for hiking. So during descent, if you come down by Monument Peak Road, you cannot continue along Service Road onto Agua Caliente Trail. You have to make a right turn to reach Calera Creek Trail at an unmarked junction.
  3. As of Feb 2018, park hours are 8:00am to sunset. So if would like to start the hike early, you'd have to find some parking on Old Calaveras Road (not sure if there is any roadside parking along that road) and walk an additional 1.4 miles round trip to Sandy Wool Lake parking lot.

Trailhead

Location: along Downing Road, Milpitas, CA 95035 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-237 East, continue onto E Calaveras Blvd. After 1.8 miles, turn left on Downing Road. Continue for 0.8 miles to reach various parking lots in Ed Levin County Park.

Parking Fees: $6 per vehicle (last visited: April 2011)

Capacity: Many cars can be parked inside the park at various parking lots.
