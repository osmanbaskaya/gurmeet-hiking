McClures Beach
2.0 miles     300 ft
7 May 2011
Summary

Peaceful hike to a long sandy beach with some rock outcrops to the south which can be climbed for great views.

Location: Point Reyes National Seashore

Elevation Profile
Trip Planning

Park Website — Yelp Reviews — Weather
Trail Maps

» Many many maps by National Park Service: Good quality maps showing various trails in different parts of Point Reyes.

» Tom Harrison Trail Map: High quality contoured map showing various trails and mileages for different sections.

Route

Route in brief: Follow the trail to McClures Beach for 0.4 miles. You may walk an additional 0.6 miles on the beach. Return by the same route.

Notes: See Yelp reviews for McClures Beach. No drinking water at trailhead or along the trail.
