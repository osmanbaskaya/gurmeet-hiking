Tolman Peak - Jordan Lake
9.8 miles     1800 ft
24 Dec 2010
Summary

A pleasant walk through green rolling hills offering panoramic valley views.

Location: Dry Creek Pioneer Regional Park

Elevation Profile
GPS

Large-sized Google Map

Trailhead

Location: along May Road, Union City, CA 94587 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From I-880 North, take exit 21 for Decoto Rd / CA-84 West toward Dumbarton Bridge. After the exit, turn right on Decoto Road and continue for 2.9 miles. Turn left on Mission Blvd and drive 0.4 miles. Take the first right onto May Road. Within 0.2 miles, there is a large parking lot on the right.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: None (last visited: December 2010)

Capacity: Almost 40 cars.

Latitude: 37.607643     Longitude: -122.018178

Route

Trailhead: "Dry Creek Garden" at the end of May Road.

Route: Dry Creek Trail (0.2 miles) → High Ridge Loop Trail → Tolman Peak Trail (0.88 miles) → South Fork Trail (1.29 miles) → Tolman Peak Trail (0.82 + 0.88 miles) → High Ridge Loop Trail → Pioneer Trail (1.19 miles) → Dry Creek Trail (0.12 + 0.70 miles) → loop around the lake to reach Entry Kiosk. Continue along Ridge View Trail (0.61 miles) → Ridge Loop Trail(1.01 miles).
