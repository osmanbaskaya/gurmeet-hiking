Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Pretty good map showing various trails.

» Redwoodhikes Trail Map: Great map showing many parks like Purisima Creek, Huddart, Wunderlich, El Corte De Madera, Foothills, Hidden Villa and Rancho San Antonio. Trail maps for individual parks are available online for free. However, the RedwoodHikes map shows the big picture and is helpful for planning hikes that go through multiple parks.

Route
From the parking lot on Skyline Blvd, follow Lost Trail (0.4 miles) → left on Hamms Gulch Trail (2.6 miles) → right on Eagle Trail (0.7 miles) → right on Razorback Ridge Trail (2.3 miles) → right on Lost Trail (2.1 miles). Eagle Trail is exceptionally pleasant after rains. It run next to a creek for about a quarter mile.
Trailhead

Location: along Skyline Blvd, 2.5 miles south of Hwy 84, 4.7 miles north of Page Mill Rd . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.
