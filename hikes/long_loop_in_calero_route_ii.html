Route
From the parking lot along McKean Road, follow Figueroa Trail (0.2 miles) → right on Los Cerritos Trail (0.4 miles) → left on Pena Trail (0.2 miles) → left on Valecito Trail (0.5 miles) → righton Figueroa Trail (0.7 miles) → left on Javelina Loop (1.5 miles) → Cottle Trail (1.3 miles) → Chisnantuck Peak Trail (1.0 miles) → right on Bald Peaks Trail → left on Catamount Trail → left on Longwall Canyon Trail → Serpentine Loop Trail (2.0 miles) → right on Canada Del Oro Cutoff (0. 2miles) → Cna Del Oro Trail (0.8 miles) → right on Figueroa Trail (1.4 miles).

Trailhead

Location: on McKean Road in San Jose, CA 95037 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 101, take the Bailey Road exit and head west 2.7 miles to McKean Road. Turn left and follow the signs to the trail head for Figueora Trail (see trail map).
