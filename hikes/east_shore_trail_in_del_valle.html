East Shore Trail in Del Valle

Summary

Great hike meandering along the east shore of Del Valle Lake. Best visited in spring when hills are lush green.

Location: Del Valle Regional Park

Route

Route in brief: Start at the Rocky Ridge Visitor Center along the west side of Del Valle Lake. Then follow West Shore Trail till you reach the bridge. After crossing the bridge, turn left along an unnamed trail. Follow signs for East Shore Trail, next to the lake. Beyond Venados Group Camp area, follow Swallow Bay Trail. Continue along Cliffs to Del Valle Regional Trail — this trail is labeled 'East Shore Trail' on the trail map but the name on signposts all along is 'Cliffs to Del Valle Regional Trail'. It is a wide fire road that takes you along rolling hills, away from the lake, all the way to "Arroyo Del Valle Staging Area" in the north. There is another trail called Heron Bay Trail. It's a single-track hiking trail that runs parallel to 'Cliffs to Del Valle Regional Trail'. You may prefer this trail if trails are not too muddy. Finally, you may also hike next to the lake itself for long stretches. You are likely to find many people fishing.

Trailhead

Start Location

Location: 7000 Del Valle Road, Livermore, CA . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Hwy 580, take exit 55 for Vasco Road towards Brentwood and drive 3.1 miles along S Vasco Road. Turn right onto Tesla Road and drive 0.8 miles. Take the first right onto Mines Road and drive 3.5 miles. Continue onto Del Valle Road for 3.2 miles to reach Del Valle park entrance kiosk.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: $6 per vehicle (last visited: February 2011)

Capacity: Dozens of cars may be parked inside the park

Latitude: 37.584174     Longitude: -121.688937

End Location

Location: 5555 Arroyo Road, Livermore, CA 94550 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-84 West in Livermore, turn right on Vallecitos Rd and drive for 4.8 miles. Turn right onto Vallecitos Road and drive for 1.1 miles. Take the 3rd right onto Wetmore Road an drive for 1.0 mile. Turn right onto Arroyo Road and drive for 2.4 miles to reach the parking lot for Del Valle Regional Park.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: None (last visited: February 2011)

Capacity: Over 20 cars may be parked here.

Latitude: 37.619454     Longitude: -121.752977
