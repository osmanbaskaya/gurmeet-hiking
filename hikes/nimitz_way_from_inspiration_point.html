Route

Route in brief: Walk along Nimitz Way (paved road) until the 4.0 mile mark (paved path ends here). Return by the same route. There are regular mile markers on the road.

Notes: Check out Yelp reviews for Inspiration Point.

Trailhead

Location: along Wildcat Canyon Rd, Orinda, CA 94563 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-24 East, take exit 7A towards Fish Ranch Rd. Merge onto Old Tunnel Road. Within 0.2 miles, merge onto Fish Ranch Rd and drive for 0.8 miles. Turn right onto Grizzly Peak Blvd and drive for 1.4 miles. Turn right onto Wildcat Canyon Rd and drive for 1.3 miles to Inspiration Point.
