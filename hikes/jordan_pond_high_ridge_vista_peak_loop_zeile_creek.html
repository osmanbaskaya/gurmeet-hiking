Jordan Pond - High Rige - Vista Peak Loop - Zeile Creek

Summary

Gorgeous hike over green rolling hills. Best visited in spring when hills are lush green.

Location: Garin Regional Park

Route

Route in Brief: Start by visiting Jordan Pond along unnamed trails (0.9 miles). Then follow High Ridge Loop Trail → Newt Pond Trail → Vista Peak Loop Trail → Zeile Creek Trail → Garin Woods Trail → Vista Peak Loop Trail → Old Homestead Road to return to parking lot.

Notes: Zeile Creek Trail and Garin Woods Trail in the northern section of the park are shaded. These trails are along Zeile Creek, making the walk pleasant after rains. At the intersection of Zeile Creek Trail and Vista Peak Loop Trail, you get great views of Oakland and Berkeley.

Trailhead

Location: along Garin Avenue, Hayward, CA 94544 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: Go along 238 North. Turn right on Garin Blvd. Drive one mile to reach the park (Garin Blvd ends at the park entrance).
