Route
Follow Priest Rock Trail (2.4 miles) to reach intersection with Limekiln Trail. Continue along Priest Rock Trail (1.4 miles) → right on Kennedy Trail (1.4 miles) to reach intersection with Limekiln Trail. Continue along Woods Trail (3.6 miles) to reach intersection with Barlow Road. Continue along Woods Trail (2.8 miles) to reach Hicks Road.
