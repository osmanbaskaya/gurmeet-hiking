Route
From the parking lot, follow Serpentine Loop Trail going west (1.7 miles) → right on Bald Peaks Trail (0.6 miles) → Canada Del Oro Trail (1.1 miles) → left on Figueroa Trail (0.1 miles) → riht on Valecito Trail (0.5 miles) → right on Pena Trail (0.2 miles) → left on Los Cerritos Trail (2.1 miles) → right on Pena Trail (0.4 miles) → Javeline Loop (0.8 miles) → Cottle Trail (1.3 miles) → Chisnantuck Peak Trail (1.0 miles) → right on Bald Peaks Trail (1.0 miles) → Longwall Canyon Trail (2.0 miles) → right on Mayfair Ranch Trail (3.1 miles) to return to parking lot.
Trailhead

Location: 4289 Casa Loma Rd Morgan Hill, CA 95037 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: (PDF) Map for Directions. From Highway 101 South, take the Bailey Avenue exit west. Then turn left onto McKean Road. Turn right onto Casa Loma Road. The staging area is at the end of the paved road on your left.
