Trailhead

Location: near 15152 Penitencia Creek Rd San Jose, CA 95132. . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Hwy 680, take Mckee Road going east. Turn left on White Road. Turn right on Penitencia Creek Road and follow this road all the way to park entrance. There is a free parking lot just before the entrance kiosk.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: Free parking just outside entrance kiosk, $6 to enter the park and park inside. (last visited: Jan 2010)

Capacity: About 20 cars can be parked outside the entrance kiosk. Dozens of cars may be parked inside the park.
