Summary

Pleasant, shaded walk through redwoods adjoining Highway 9.

Location: Castle Rock State Park — Saratoga Gap Open Space Preserve

Route
From Saratoga Gap trailhead at the intersection of Hwy 9 and Hwy 35, follow Skyline Trail going east (1.6 miles) → right on Loghry Woods Trail (1.8 miles) → right on Service Road Trail (0.3 miles) → right on Frog Flat Trail (0.6 miles) → left on Saratoga Gap Trail (0.4 miles) → left on Travertine Springs Trail (2.1 miles) → right on Saratoga Toll Road (2.0 miles) → right on Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.6 miles).

Trailhead

Location: Intersection of Highway 9 and Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd) . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 85, take Exit 14 for Saratoga Ave going south. After 1.8 miles, continue along Big Basin Way (Highway 9) for 7.3 miles to reach the junction with Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd)
