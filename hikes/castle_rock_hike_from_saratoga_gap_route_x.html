Castle Rock Hike from Saratoga Gap - Route X

Summary

Pleasant, shaded walk through redwoods adjoining Highway 9 and Highway 35.

Location: Castle Rock State Park — Saratoga Gap Open Space Preserve

Route

Saratoga Gap / Long Ridge (3.8 miles): From the trail head, follow Saratoga Gap Trail (1.7 miles) → Bay Area Ridge Trail (0.3 miles) to reach Highway 35. Cross over the highway. Then enter Long Ridge Open Space Preserve. Follow Bay Area Ridge Trail (0.1 miles) → Achistaca Trail (1.7 miles).

To Beekhuis Road Trail (3.0 miles): Turn right on Skyline to the Sea Trail (1.5 miles). Cross Highway 9 again to continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (1.5 miles). At this point, there is a junction with Beekhuis Road Trail. In Nov 2012, this junction was unmarked. We continued, hoping to reach 'Cutoff Trail', which was shown on the VirtualParks trail map. However, we never came across any such junction. Turning left on the unmarked junction with Beekhuis Road Trail is important, otherwise, you will continue walking on Skyline to the Sea Trail for 2.6 miles to reach Waterman Gap Trail Camp.

To Trailhead (3.7 miles): Turn left and continue along Beekhuis Road Trail (0.4 miles) and read the signs carefully at the junction with Saratoga Toll Road. Turn left on Saratoga Toll Road (0.8 miles) to reach a junction with Travertine Springs Trail. Continue along Saratoga Toll Road (2.0 miles) → continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.5 miles) to return to trail head. In Nov 2012, we noticed an unmarked intersection along Saratoga Toll Road. Going south to north, it was obvious which option to follow. Going north to south, it would not have been so obvious.

Trailhead

Location: Intersection of Highway 9 and Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd) . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 85, take Exit 14 for Saratoga Ave going south. After 1.8 miles, continue along Big Basin Way (Highway 9) for 7.3 miles to reach the junction with Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd)
