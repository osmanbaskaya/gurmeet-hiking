Route

To Berry Creek Falls (6.8 miles): From Park HQ, follow Skyline to the Sea Trail (2.9 miles) → turn right on Timms Creek Trail (0.9 miles) → left on Sunset Trail (2.0 miles) → left on Berry Creek Falls Trail (1.0 miles).

To Park HQ (5.3 miles): From Berry Creek Falls, follow Howard King Trail (4.4 miles) to reach a five-way intersection with Middle Ridge Road and Skyline to the Sea Trail. Howard King Trail and Hammond Road are intertwined. So there are repeated points of intersection between the two trails. In fact, a section of Howard King Trail is identical to Hammond Road. Eventually, Howard King Trail meets Skyline to the Sea Trail. Turn right and follow Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.9 miles) to reach Park HQ.

Notes: Near Park HQ, there are many trails. This map is helpful.
