Short Loop in Edgewood

Summary

Nice short loop through green hills and wildflowers in spring.

Location: Edgewood County Park

Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Good map showing various trails.

» Redwoodhikes Trail Map: Great map showing many parks like Purisima Creek, Huddart, Wunderlich, El Corte De Madera, Foothills, Hidden Villa and Rancho San Antonio. Trail maps for individual parks are available online for free. However, the RedwoodHikes map shows the big picture and is helpful for planning hikes that go through multiple parks.

Route
From the parking lot, follow Sylvan Trail (0.1 miles) → Sylvan Loop Trail (0.9 miles) → Serpentine Loop Trail (1.5 miles) → Old Stage Road (0.8 miles).
Trailhead

Location: 10 Old Stage Coach Rd, Redwood City, CA 94062 . Parking area is not visible in Google Maps.

Directions: From I-280 North, take exit 29 for Edgewood Rd, turn right on Edgewood Rd and drive for 0.9 miles. Take the first right onto Old Stage Coach Road to enter Edgewood Park.
