Trailhead

Location: Intersection of Anton Way & Alves Drive, Cupertino, CA 95014 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-85 South, take exit 18 for Stevens Creek Blvd, turn left onto Stevens Creek Blvd and drive for 0.5 miles. Make a U-turn at S Stellings Road and drive for 0.2 miles. Take the first right onto Anton Way.
