Summary

Nice walk through marshlands and sloughs without any elevation gain. Especially awesome at sunrise and sunset.

Location: Palo Alto Baylands

Route

The route is particularly scenic early morning or just before sunset. Shadows of clouds in the water and the presence of many birds makes it a peaceful experience in nature.

Follow a paved road for about a quarter mile, ignoring a paved road that joins from the left and another that joins from the right. The road soon becomes a dirt road and meanders next to sloughs and marshlands. Keep following signs for Adobe Creek Trail until reaching a bridge, where the trail makes a sharp left. On the right, there is a bench for admiring nature. Retrace your steps back to the parking lot from this point.

Trailhead

Location: Intersection of San Antonio Road and Terminal Boulevard, Mountain View, CA 94043 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 101, take San Antonio North exit. Within 0.3 miles, San Antonio Road makes a sharp right onto Terminal Road. Plenty of street parking on Terminal Road.
