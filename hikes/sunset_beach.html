Summary

A pleasant walk along Home Bay and Drakes Estero in Point Reyes.

Location: Point Reyes National Seashore

Route

To Sunset Beach (4.7 miles): From Estero Trailhead, follow Estero Trail (2.5 miles) → Sunset Beach Trail (1.6 miles) to reach Sunset Beach. The beach is muddy. Continuing south, the beach becomes rocky. If the tide permits, walk all along the rocky section of the beach (0.6 miles round trip) to get views of Limantour Spit and the Pacific Ocean.

To Estero Trailhead (4.1 miles): Retrace your steps from Sunset Beach to Estero Trailhead.
