Route
Walk all along the shoreline. In the north, we can walk all the way to the "Fishing Pier" beyond Port View Park. In the south, we can walk up to "Western Pacific Mole" shown on the map.

Trailhead

Location: 7th Street & Middle Harbor Road, Oakland, CA . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From I-880 North, take exit 44 for 7th St towards W Grand Ave, then turn left onto 7th St. Within 1.0 mile, the intersection with Middle Harbor Road marks the entrance to Middle Harbor Shoreline.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: None (last visited: Jan 2011)

Capacity: Dozens of cars may be parked inside the park.
