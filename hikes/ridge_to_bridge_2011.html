Route

The 31-mile Ridge to Bridge route was all along the Bay Area Ridge Trail.

Starting point: Sir Francis Drake Blvd where Bolinas Ridge Trail starts.

Ending point: North of Golden Gate Bridge (Conzelman Road parking lot in Marin Headlands).

Trailhead
