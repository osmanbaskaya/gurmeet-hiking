» Official East Bay Parks Trail Map: Good map showing various hiking trails. Please see Text Portion of the map too.

Route

To Sacramento River: Follow an unnamed trail all the way to Sacramento River. In May 2012, the last few hundred yards of the trail meandered through marshlands, making shoes wet. Return by the same route to return to parking lot.

Harrier Trail Loop: Follow Harrier Trail from the parking lot. It forms a lollipop shaped loop, returning to the parking lot.
