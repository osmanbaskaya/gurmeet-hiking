Trailhead

Location: near the eastern end of Embarcadero Road in Palo Alto, CA . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 101, take Embarcadero Rd exit. Follow signs for Embarcadero Road East. After 0.8 miles, turn left to stay on Embarcadero Rd East. Within 0.3 miles, there is a prominent sign for Palo Alto Duck Pond.
