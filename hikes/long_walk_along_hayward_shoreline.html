Route

Trailhead: Hayward Shoreline Interpretive Center.

Route: Follow SF Bay Trail all the way to San Lorenzo Creek. Turn right along a semi-paved trail parallel to the creek. The trail turns right to reach Grant Ave. On Grant Avenue, there is a restaurant called Luisa's Perubian Food & Bar: 2541 Grant Avenue, San Lorenzo, CA 94580. Return by the same route.

Trailhead

Location: Hayward Shoreline Interpretive Center, 4901 Breakwater Avenue, Hayward, CA 94545 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-92 West, take exit 24 for Clawiter Rd toward Eden Landing Rd. In 0.3 miles, merge onto Breakwater Ave. Within 1.0 mile, Hayward Shoreline Interpretive Center is on the right.
