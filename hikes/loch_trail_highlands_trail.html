Loch Trail - Highlands Trail
5.0 miles     800 ft
29 May 2010
Summary

Great hike through forests and trails meandering next to Loch Lomond Lake. Extensive picnic area near the trailhead.

Location: Loch Lomond Recreational Area

Elevation Profile
Trip Planning

Park Website — Yelp Reviews — Weather
Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Decent quality map. Lengths of trail sections not shown. In May 2010, the trail map available at entrance kiosk had more details than the online map.

Route

Start at the Park Store, which is close to Loch View Picnic Area. Then follow Loch Trail all along the lake. Then take Highlands Trail to return. You may take a side trip to Big Trees Trail also. See trail map for details.
