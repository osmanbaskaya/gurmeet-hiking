Route

Trailhead: Parking lot near park headquarters.

Route in brief: From the parking lot near Sanborn Park HQ, follow Sanborn Trail (1.0 + 1.2 miles) → San Andreas Trail (1.5 + 0.4 + 0.4 miles) to parking lot.

Trailhead

Location: near Youth Science Institute, 16055 Sanborn Road, Saratoga, CA 95070 . Parking area is not visible in Google Maps.

Directions: From Hwy 85, take exit 14 for Saratoga Ave, going south and drive 1.8 miles. Continue onto CA-9 (Big Basin Way) and drive 2.5 miles. Turn left onto Sanborn Road and drive 0.7 miles to reach Park HQ.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: $6 vehicle entrance fees after 8am (last visited: July 2010)

Capacity: Dozens of cars may be parked inside the park.
