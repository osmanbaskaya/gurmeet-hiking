Drakes Head and Sunset Beach
12.8 miles     1000 ft

Summary

A pleasant walk along Home Bay and Drakes Estero in Point Reyes. Plus a trip to awesome views of Limantour Spit from Drakes Head.

Location: Point Reyes National Seashore

Route

To Sunset Beach (4.7 miles): From Estero Trailhead, follow Estero Trail (2.5 miles) → Sunset Beach Trail (1.6 miles) to reach Sunset Beach. The beach is muddy. Continuing south, the beach becomes rocky. If the tide permits, walk all along the rocky section of the beach (0.6 miles round trip) to get views of Limantour Spit and the Pacific Ocean.

To Drakes Head (3.6 miles): From Sunset Beach, walk back along Sunset Beach Trail (1.6 miles) → right on Estero Trail (0.7 miles) → right on Draks Head (1.3 miles) to reach Drakes Head. In August 2013, the signs along these trails were sketchy. The trail was visible but faint at many places. However, we never felt lost. The views of Limantour Spit from Drakes Head are just superb! One of the best views in bay area, California.

To Estero Trailhead (4.5 miles): From Drakes Head, walk back along Drakes Head Trail (1.3 miles) → left on Estero Trail (0.7 miles) to reach intersection with Sunset Beach Trail → right on Estero Trail (2.5 miles) to return to Estero Trailhead.

Notes: All in all, a pleasant hike in overcast conditions. Almost all the trails are in the open, so avoid summers. Views from Drakes Head are superb! In August 2013, we ran into many clusters of cows along the trail. Along the hike, views of Home Bay, Drakes Estero and Estero de Limantour are great!

Trailhead
