Summary

Walk along the entire length of Golden Gate Bridge with awesome views of Angel Island and San Francisco skyline.

Location: Marin Headlands

Route
From the Vista Point parking lot in the northern end of Golden Gate Bridge, walk in the sidewalk to the east of the bridge all the way to the southern end (1.8 miles). You may then follow bike trails for an additional 0.4 miles round-trip. Then walk back along the bridge to the starting point.

Trailhead

Location: 'Vista Point' Parking Lot at the northern end of Golden Gate Bridge, CA . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From San Francisco, drive over Golden Gate Bridge going north. The first exit beyond the bridge is for a 'Vista Point'.

Google Maps:

Parking Fees: None (last visited: June 2012)

Capacity: Over 50 cars may be parked here. Parking is usually tight in peak hours.
