Route
From the 0.72-mile marker along Welch Creek Road (see map for its location), follow Lower Maguire Peaks Trail (0.8 miles) → left on Maguire Peaks Loop Trail (3.2 miles) → left on Lower Maguire Peaks Trail (0.8 miles) to return to the parking lot. Make a side trip to Maguire Peaks (0.4 miles round-trip). For the side trip, the trail is not shown on the map, see GPS tracks instead.
