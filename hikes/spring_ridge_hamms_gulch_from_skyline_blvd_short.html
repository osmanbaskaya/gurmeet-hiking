Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Pretty good map showing various trails.

» Redwoodhikes Trail Map: Great map showing many parks like Purisima Creek, Huddart, Wunderlich, El Corte De Madera, Foothills, Hidden Villa and Rancho San Antonio. Trail maps for individual parks are available online for free. However, the RedwoodHikes map shows the big picture and is helpful for planning hikes that go through multiple parks.

Route
From Skyline Blvd parking lot, turn left on Bay Area Ridge Trail going north (0.1 miles) → right on Anniversary Trail (0.7 miles) to reach a second parking lot on Skyline Blvd. Turn right on Spring Ridge Trail (1.6 miles) → right on Meadow Trail (0.7 miles) → right on unknown trail (0.4 miles) → right on Hamms Gulch Trail (2.6 miles) → right on Bay Area Ridge Trail (0.4 miles) to return to the parking lot on Skyline Blvd.
