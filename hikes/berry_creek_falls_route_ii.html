Route

To Berry Creek Falls: Hop onto Skyline to the Sea Trail from Park HQ. There are lots of trails near Park HQ: this map is helpful. Keep following Skyline to the Sea Trail until you reach a junction with Berry Creek Falls Trail. Berry Creek falls is about 0.1 miles along Berry Creek Falls Trail.

To Park HQ: From Berry Creek Falls, retrace your steps to Skyline to the Sea Trail, following it until you reach Howard King Trail. Then hop onto Howard King Trail, which climbs quickly. Turn left on Hammond Road unti you reach a bench facing the Pacific Ocean — this spot is McAbee Overlook. Within a few feet of this bench, turn right onto Howard King Trail. Continue along Howard King Trail — it intersects Hammond Road, a fire road, many times (see trail map). Howard King Trail ends at a five-way intersection. At tthis point, hop onto Skyline to the Sea Trail back to Park HQ.
