Summary

Route

To Intersection of Hwy 9 and Hwy 236 (6.6 miles): From the trailhead at the intersection of Hwy 9 and Hwy 35 (Skyline Blvd), start walking south along Hwy 9. Within 0.1 miles, there is a sign for Skyline to the Sea Trail on the left. Follow Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.5 miles). Then cross Highway 9 to continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail on the other side of Highway 9 (1.5 miles). Cross Highway 9 again to continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (1.5 miles). At this point, there is a junction with Beekhuis Road Trail. In Nov 2012, this junction was unmarked. Continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (3.0 miles) to reach the intersection of Hwy 9 and Hwy 236.

To Beekhuis Road Trail (5.2 miles): From the intersection of Hwy 236 and Hwy 9, find signposts for Skyline to the Sea Trail going south towards Big Basin Park HQ. Follow Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.6 miles) → left on a connector trail that goes to Saratoga Toll Road (1.5 miles). The connector trail crosses Hwy 9. Upon reaching Hwy 9, turn left and walk a few steps to see a signpost marking the connector trail. Continue along the Connector Trail (0.2 miles), then turn left on Saratoga Toll Road (2.9 miles) to reach an intersection with Beekhuis Road Trail.

To Trailhead (3.3 miles): Continue along Saratoga Toll Road (0.8 miles) to reach a junction with Travertine Springs Trail. Continue along Saratoga Toll Road (2.0 miles) to reach an intersection with Skyline to the Sea Trail. Continue along Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.5 miles) to return to trail head.

Notes: Along Saratoga Toll Road, near the intersection with Beekhuis Road Trail, there were some unmarked intersections in Dec 2012. We followed the strategy of avoided trails which were losing elevation quickly, and didn't get lost. There were unmarked intersections along Saratoga Toll Road close to the trailhead as well.
