Route

Route in brief: Upon entering the park through the kiosk on Junipera Blvd, a paved trail quickly climbs up to reach an intersection. Follow this road — it forms aloop. At about the mid-way pont, Stanford Dish is visible. When the loop is over, take right to return to the starting point.

Notes: The entire hike is on a wide paved road. Hardly any shade. Great workout. No restrooms anywhere. No trailmap available — the hike is a simple loop. Finally, make sure you study Dish website for park hours, which are strictly enforced. Entrance is closed at sunset.

Trailhead

Location: Intersection of Stanford Ave & Junipero Serra Blvd, Palo Alto, CA 94305 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.
