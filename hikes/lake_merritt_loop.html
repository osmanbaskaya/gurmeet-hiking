Route

Route in brief: Just walk around the lake.

Notes: Restrooms are available at Lake Merritt Rowing Club which is on the north side of Lake Merritt.

Trailhead

Location: near the intersection of Lakeshore Ave & Foothill Blvd, Oakland, CA 94606 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From I-880 North, take exit 40 for Embarcadero towards 5th Avenue, turn right on Embarcadero, then turn right onto 5th Avenue and drive for 0.7 miles. Turn left onto Foothill Blvd and drive for 0.3 miles to reach the intersection with Lakeshore Ave. There are several parking spots next to the lake.
