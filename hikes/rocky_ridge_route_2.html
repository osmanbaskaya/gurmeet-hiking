Route

Rocky Ridge Loop (4.7 miles): From the Staging Area, follow Rocky Ridge View Trail (0.5 miles) → left on Cuesta Trail (1.4 miles) → left on Rocky Ridge View Trail (0.4 miles) to reach an intersection with Sycamore Trail. Continue along Rocky Ridge View Trail (0.3 miles) → left on Elderberry Trail (1.7 miles) → left on Bollinger Creek Loop Trail (0.4 miles) to return to the Stging Area.

Notes:

  • Rocky Ridge View Trail is fairly steep. It climbs up 500 ft in the first half mile. To do this section in the end, you may reverse the overall loop.
  • Near the intersection of Devil's Hole Trail and Rocky Ridge View Trail, there is a high point (1893 ft) shown on the map. The views from this point are almost 360 degrees.
