Summary

Short walk to a church with fabulous views of nearby dwellings and San Francisco Bay.

Location: Old St Hilary's Open Space Preserve

Route

Trailhead: Cul-de-sac on Vistazo West Drive.

Route: Follow Vistazo Fire Rd all the way. Make a side trip to Old St Hilary's Church. Return by same route. This is a pleasant short walk with great views of San Francisco Bay, Golden Gate Bridge and homes in the nearby valley.

Trailhead

Location: near 1498 Vistazo W St, Tiburon, Marin, California 94920 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From US-101 North, take exit 447 for CA-131 toward Tiburon Blvd / E Blithedale Ave. Then turn right onto CA-131 East / Tiburon Blvd and drive for 3.5 miles. Turn left on Lyford Drive and drive for 0.4 miles. Turn right onto Vistazo Street West and drive for 0.2 miles to reach an entrance for Old St Hilary's.
