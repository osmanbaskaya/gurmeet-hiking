Route

To Castle Rock Park HQ (9.1 miles): From Saratoga Gap parking lot, follow Skyline to the Sea Trail (0.5 miles) → Saratoga Toll Road (2.3 miles) → left on Travertine Springs Trail (2.0 miles) → right Saratoga Gap Trail (3.3 miles) → right on Castle Rock Loop (1.0 miles) to reach Castle Rock Park HQ.

To Saratoga Gap Trailhead (3.1 miles): From Castle Rock Park HQ, cross over to the other side of Hwy 35 and turn left onto Skyline Trail (3.1 miles) to reach Saratoga Gap trailhead.

Trailhead

Location: Intersection of Highway 9 and Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd) . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 85, take Exit 14 for Saratoga Ave going south. After 1.8 miles, continue along Big Basin Way (Highway 9) for 7.3 miles to reach the junction with Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd)
