Coastal Hike from Fort Funston to Cliff House
5.3 miles     200 ft
23 Apr 2011
Summary

Nice walk along a five-mile sandy beach extending from Fort Funston in the south to Cliff House in the north.

Location: Fort Funston — San Francisco

Elevation Profile

Large-sized Google Map

Trip Planning

Park Website — Yelp Reviews — Weather
Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Good map showing various trails. Mileage information is missing.

» Walker's Map of San Francisco by Pease Press: Excellent quality maps showing many parks and trails in the San Francisco area. Many of these parks do not have free trail maps online. Cost was $7.95 in April 2011.

Route

Route in Brief: om the parking lot, walk towards the ocean to a lookout point. Turn right and walk along unnamed trails headed north. Soon, the trail drops down to the beach. Continue walking until the beach ends at Cliff House. Return by the same route.
