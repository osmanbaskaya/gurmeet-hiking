Coastal Hike from Mussel Rock to Cliff House
9.0 miles     400 ft
23 Apr 2011
Summary

Nice walk along a long nine-mile sandy beach extending from Mussel Rock in the south to Cliff House in the north.

Location: Mussel Rock Park — Fort Funston — San Francisco

Start Location

Location: Cul-de-sac on Westline Drive, Daly City, CA 94015 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-1 South, take exit 507 for Palmetto Ave toward Manor Ave, keep right at the fork and follow signs for Monterey Road / Palmetto Ave, turn right on Palmetto Ave and drive for 0.7 miles. Turn left onto Westline Drive and drive for 0.4 miles. On Westline Drive, there is a fork in the road. To the right is Allied Waste Services. To the left lies the parking lot for Mussel Rock Park. Studying Google Maps would be helpful.

Route

Route:

  • From the parking lot, start walking north. Look for a trail going down to the beach. Trails are unnamed. No signs. Study Google Map in satellite view to get an idea of trails in Mussel Rock Park.
  • Walk 9 miles along a long sandy beach that ends at the Cliff House in the north. At the 4 mile mark, you may climb up a sand ladder to explore Fort Funston which has a hang gliding area. Then continue walking north along the cliffs in Fort Funston until a trail going left takes you back to the beach. Continue along the beach until the very north where you may explore a small cave that fills with water at high tide. Then walk a short distance back (0.2 miles) to the parking lot.
Mussel Rock City Park

Plenty of wildflowers near the parking lot, before descending down the beach.
