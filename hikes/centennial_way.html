Route
From South San Francisco BART station, walk all along Centennial Way, a paved bike trail until it reaches San Bruno BART Station. Return by the same route. This is an urban walk, going through some traffic intersections like Chestnut Blvd, W Orange Ave and S Spruce Ave.
Trailhead

Start Location

Location: BART & Mission Road, South San Francisco, CA 94080 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From El Camino Real (CA-82) going north in South San Francisco, turn right onto Chestnut Ave and drive for 0.2 miles. Turn left onto Mission Rd and drive for 0.7 miles to reach an intersection with BART. There is plenty of street parking along Mission Blvd. Walk along the BART entrance. The trailhead for Centennial Way is to the south of the BART station (see Google Maps or GPS Tracks for its location).
