Rose Peak from Del Valle Lake
20.2 miles     4500 ft
14 Feb 2010
Summary

A strenuous hike over rugged terrain and green rolling hills offering majestic valley views.

Location: Ohlone Regional Wilderness

» Ohlone 50K Ultra Marathon Route: Official Trail Map is part of the permit that is needed to walk along Ohlone Wilderness Trail. The Ohlone50K website has a series of maps that show the entire trail.

» Online Permit Application: The permit includes a large-sized trail map.

Route

Permit: Make sure you have the permit to use Ohlone Wilderness Trail. Permits are available at entrance kiosk and online.

Route in brief: Start from the parking lot near Ardilla Group Camp and West Swim Beach. Follow Sailor Camp Trail for about one mile. Then follow Ohlone Wilderness Trail all the way to Rose Peak which offers excellent 360-degree views. The first 5.5 miles are very steep.

Murietta Falls: Murietta Falls is a seasonal waterfall that has plenty of water after recent rains. Visiting the falls requires a detour. You have to turn right at a signpost numbered "35" — Murietta Falls is clearly marked at this signpost. See trail map for details. After Murietta Falls, take Greenside Road8 to rejoin Ohlone Wilderness Trail. This detour adds about 1 mile to the hike.

Trailhead

Location: 7000 Del Valle Road, Livermore, CA . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Hwy 580, take exit 55 for Vasco Road towards Brentwood and drive 3.1 miles along S Vasco Road. Turn right onto Tesla Road and drive 0.8 miles. Take the first right onto Mines Road and drive 3.5 miles. Continue onto Del Valle Road for 3.2 miles to reach Del Valle park entrance kiosk.
