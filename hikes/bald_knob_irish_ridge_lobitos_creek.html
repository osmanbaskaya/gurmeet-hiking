Bald Knob - Irish Ridge - Lobitos Creek
8.0 miles     1400 ft
27 Jun 2010
Summary

An interesting hike through redwoods and green rolling hills in a rarely visited part of Purisima Creek. In June 2010, some bushwhacking required towards the end.

Location: Purisima Creek Redwoods Open Space Preserve

» Redwoodhikes Trail Map: Great map showing many parks like Purisima Creek, Huddart, Wunderlich, El Corte De Madera, Foothills, Hidden Villa and Rancho San Antonio. Trail maps for individual parks are available online for free. However, the RedwoodHikes map shows the big picture and is helpful for planning hikes that go through multiple parks.

Route

Route: Grabtown Gulch Trail (0.3 miles) → Borden Hatch Mill Trail (0.2 miles) → Bald Knob Trail (1.3 miles) → Irish Ridge Trail (1.2 miles) → Lobitos Creek Trail (~ 1.1 miles). Return by same route.

Notes:

  1. Lobitos Creek Trail is the most scenic, amid cool redwoods. Beyond the 1-mile mark, the trail is unmaintained — it's best to turn back unless you are comfortable with bush-whacking and confident that you can find your way back.
  2. Bald Knob and Irish Ridge Trail are in the open, unshaded — avoid summers for this hike.
  3. Details by WeeklyWalkter
  4. No facilities or maps available at trail head. You may pick maps from the main Park Entrance on Skyline Blvd (see official trail map for its location).
  5. This section of the park is relatively quiet — you are likely to meet very few hikers.
Trailhead

Location: along Tunitas Creek Rd, Redwood City, CA 94062 . No street address. Parking area is not visible in Google Maps.

Directions: From 280 North, take 84 West. Right on Kings Mountain Rd. Drive all the way to intersection with Highway 35. Cross the highway to continue along Tunitas Creek Rd. In 2.0 miles, The trailhead would be on your right (marked "PC04"). Alternately, from Highway 92, go 6.5 miles south on Highway 35 (Skyline Boulevard). Turn right (west) onto Tunitas Creek Road. About 2 miles down the road, the trail head is marked "PC04".

Parking Fees: None (last visited: Jan 2012)
