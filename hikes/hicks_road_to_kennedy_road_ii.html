Route
Follow Woods Trail (2.8 miles) to reach intersection with Barlow Road. Continue along Woods Trail (3.6 miles) → left on Limekiln Trail (2.9 miles) → right on Priest Rock Trail (1.4 miles) → left on Kennedy Trail (3.8 miles) to reach Kennedy Trail trailhead.
