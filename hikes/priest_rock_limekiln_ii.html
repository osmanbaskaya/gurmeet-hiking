Route

Route in brief: Priest Rock Trail (2.4 miles) to reach an intersection with Limekiln Trail. Continue along Priest Rock Trail (1.4 miles) to reach an intersection with Kennedy Trail. Retrace your steps along Priest Rock Trail (1.4 miles) → right on Limekiln Trail (2.2 miles) → Alma Bridge Road (0.4 miles).

Trailhead

Location: along Alma Bridge Road, near Lexington Reservoir, Los Gatos, CA 95033 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-17 South, take Bear Creek Road Exit. Turn right onto Bear Creek Rd / Gillian Cichowski Memorial Overcrossing. Turn left onto CA-17 North ramp to Alma Bridge Rd / San Jose. Merge onto CA-17 N. Within half a mile, turn right onto Alma Bridge Road. Continue driving 1.6 miles, past Lexington Reservoir parking lot, to the point where Priest Rock Trail starts. About 5 to 6 cars may be parked in front of the trailhead. There is a large parking lot called 'Banjo Point', about 0.3 miles south of the trailhead: see Trail Map for Lexington Reservoir for its location.
