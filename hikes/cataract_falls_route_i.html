Cataract Falls - Route I

Summary

A gorgeous but steep hike next to a creek that is full of water after rains.

Location: Mount Tamalpais Watershed

Route
Follow Cataract Falls Trail all the way to Laurel Dell which has a shaded picnic area for a relaxed meal.
Trailhead

Location: (Cataract Falls Trailhead) along Bolinas Fairfax Road in Fairfax, CA 94930 . No street address. Parking area is not visible in Google Maps.

Directions: In Fairfax, go along Bolinas Road going west and drive 2.5 miles. Continue along Bolinas Fairfax Road and drive 5.7 miles to reach a pullout along the road. This pullout is at a hairpin bend in Bolinas Fairfax Road, very close to Alpine Lake. There is a sign for Cataract Falls near the trailhead.
