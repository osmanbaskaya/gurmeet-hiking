Summary

A gorgeous walk along sandy beaches, bluff trails and bike trails in Half Moon Bay.

Location: Half Moon Bay State Beach

Route
Walk along any combination of the beach, a paved bike trail or along single-track trails on cliffs next to the ocean. There are food shops and restrooms at Pillar Point Harbor where you may take a break. Access from the trail head to Redondo Beach is quite steep.
Trailhead

Location: Intersection of Redondo Beach Road & Thone Ave, Half Moon Bay, CA 94019 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From CA-92 West, turn left onto CA-1 South and drive 1.9 miles. Turn right onto Redondo Beach Road and drive 0.8 miles to the parking lot for Redondo Beach.
