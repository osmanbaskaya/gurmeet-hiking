Loch Trail
3.4 miles     200 ft

Summary

Scenic hike all along a trail meandering next to Lake Lomond. Nice picnic spots next to the trailhead.

Location: Loch Lomond Recreational Area

Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Decent quality map. Lengths of trail sections not shown. In May 2010, the trail map available at entrance kiosk had more details than the online map.

Route

Start at the Park Store, which is close to Loch View Picnic Area. Then follow Loch Trail all along the lake as long as it meanders next to the lake. After about 1.7 miles, the trail starts climbing up away from the lake. At this point, retrace your steps back to the trail head.
