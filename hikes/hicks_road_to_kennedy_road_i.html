Route
Follow Woods Trail (2.8 miles) to reach intersection with Barlow Road. Continue along Woods Trail (3.6 miles) to reach intersection with Limekiln Trail. Continue along Kennedy Trail (1.4 miles) to reach intersection with Priest Rock Trail. Continue along Kennedy Trail (3.8 miles) to reach Kennedy Trail trailhead.
