Route
Mayfair Ranch Trail (3.1 miles) → Longwall Canyon Trail (2.0 miles) to picnic tables and vista point. Bald Peaks Trail (1.0 miles) → Catamount Trail (1.7 miles) → Serpentine Loop Trail (0.7 miles).
Trailhead

Location: 4289 Casa Loma Rd Morgan Hill, CA 95037 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: (PDF) Map for Directions. From Highway 101 South, take the Bailey Avenue exit west. Then turn left onto McKean Road. Turn right onto Casa Loma Road. The staging area is at the end of the paved road on your left.
