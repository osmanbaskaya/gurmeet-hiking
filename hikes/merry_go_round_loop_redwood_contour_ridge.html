Trailhead

Location: close to Intersection of Blackhawk Canyon Road & Highway 152, Gilroy, CA 95020 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: Hwy 101 South → Hwy 152 West → drive about 5 miles to reach intersection with Blackhawk Road. Sprig Recreation Area is about 0.2 miles further along Hwy 152.
