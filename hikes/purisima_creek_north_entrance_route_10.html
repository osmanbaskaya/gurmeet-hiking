Summary

A long loop through all three park entrances: east, north and west.

Location: Purisima Creek Redwoods Open Space Preserve

Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: High quality map.

» Redwoodhikes Trail Map: Great map showing many parks like Purisima Creek, Huddart, Wunderlich, El Corte De Madera, Foothills, Hidden Villa and Rancho San Antonio. Trail maps for individual parks are available online for free. However, the RedwoodHikes map shows the big picture and is helpful for planning hikes that go through multiple parks.

Route

To West Entrance (3.6 miles): From North Entrance on Skyline Blvd, follow North Ridge Trail (0.5 miles) → Harkins Ridge Trail (3.0 miles) to reach the West Entrance for Purisima Creek Redwoods.

To East Entrance (4.1 miles): From West Entrance on Higgins Canyon Road, follow Purisima Creek Trail (4.1 miles) to East Entrance on Skyline Blvd.

To North Entrance (5.6 miles): From East Entrance on Skyline Blvd, follow Purisima Creek Trail (1.8 miles) → right on Craig Britton Trail (2.6 miles) → right on Harkins Ridge Trail (1.2 miles) to reach North Entrance on Skyline Blvd.

Trailhead

Location: along CA-35 (Skyline Blvd), 4.4 miles south of intersection with CA-92 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From the intersection of CA-92 and CA-35 (Skyline Blvd), go 4.4 miles south along CA-35 to reach a large parking lot for Purisima Creek Redwoods.
