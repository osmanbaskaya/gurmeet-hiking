Route

Trailhead: Parking lot near Park HQ.

To Sunnyvale Mountain Parking Lot (4.1 miles): From the parking lot near Sanborn Park HQ, follow Sanborn Trail (1.0 + 1.2 + 0.6 miles) → Skyline Trail (1.3 miles) to Sunnyvale Mountain parking lot on Highway 35.

To Sanborn Park HQ (4.2 miles): From Sunnyvale Mountain parking lot, return by Skyline Trail (1.3 miles) → Sanborn Trail (0.6 miles) → San Andreas Trail (1.5 + 0.4 + 0.4 miles) to parking lot.

Notes: The hike may be started at the Sunnyvale Mountain parking lot (free parking) on Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd). See trail map for its location.

Trailhead

Location: near Youth Science Institute, 16055 Sanborn Road, Saratoga, CA 95070 . Parking area is not visible in Google Maps.

Directions: From Hwy 85, take exit 14 for Saratoga Ave, going south and drive 1.8 miles. Continue onto CA-9 (Big Basin Way) and drive 2.5 miles. Turn left onto Sanborn Road and drive 0.7 miles to reach Park HQ.
