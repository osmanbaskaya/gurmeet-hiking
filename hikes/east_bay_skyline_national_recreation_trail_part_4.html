Route

Route in brief: Skyline Gate Staging Area (entrance for Redwood Regional Park) to MacDonald Gate Staging Area (an entrance for Anthony Chabot Regional Park). There are two choices. The GPS tracks and elevation profile shown in this article are for choice (a) that goes mostly along West Ridge Trail.

  1. 5.0 mile route: West Ridge Trail (3.83 miles) → Golden Spike Trail (0.67 miles) → connector (0.11 miles) → Big Bear Staging Area → MacDonald Trail (0.4 miles) to MacDonald Gate Staging Area.
  2. 6.5 mile route: West Ridge Trail (0.59 miles) → French Trail (3.99 miles) → Orchard Trail (0.20 miles) → West Ridge Trail (0.57 miles) → Golden Spike Trail (0.67 miles) → connector (0.11 miles) → Big Bear Staging Area → MacDonald Trail (0.4 miles) to MacDonald Gate Staging Area.

Notes:

  1. French Trail is absolutely fantastic with several ups and downs through Redwoods.
  2. Golden Spike Trail is shaded.
  3. Details in pages 60-63 in East Bay Trails: Hiking Trails in Alameda and Contra Costa Counties (288 pages, 2005) by David Weintraub.
