Route

Route in brief: Starting at MacDonald Gate Staging Area for Anthony Chabot Regional Park, follow McDonald Trail (2.4 miles) → right onto a connector just before Bort Meadow Staging Area (0.1 miles) → left onto Grass Valley Trail (1.5) miles to reach 'Stone Bridge' → left onto Brandon Trail (5.0 miles)→ right to a trail that meanders next to Redwood Road (0.4 miles) to reach Proctor Staging Area.

Notes: Details in pages 64-68 in East Bay Trails: Hiking Trails in Alameda and Contra Costa Counties (288 pages, 2005) by David Weintraub. The route is gently graded along wide trails, mostly in the open.

End Location

Location: (Proctor Staging Area) along Redwood Rd, Castro Valley, CA 94546 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From I-580 East, take exit 36A for Redwood Rd toward Castro Valley. In 0.3 miles, turn left onto Redwood Road. In 2.2 miles, Proctor Staging Area is on the left.
