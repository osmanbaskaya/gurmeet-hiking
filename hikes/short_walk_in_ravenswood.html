Short Walk in Ravenswood

Summary

A pleasant walk in a rather secluded park next to San Francisco Bay.

Location: Ravenswood Open Space Preserve

Elevation Profile
Trip Planning

Park Website — Yelp Reviews — Weather
Trail Maps

» Official Trail Map: Good quality map showing various trails. Bay Trail connects Ravenswood to Baylands Nature Preserve (not shown on trail map)

Route
Follow paved trail shown on map for 0.1 miles, cross a bridge, then follow Bay Trail for 1.2 miles to reach a lookout point. Return by same route.

Trailhead

Location: 2136 Bay Road, East Palo Alto, CA 94303 . Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Hwy 101, take University Ave exit, go north along University Ave for 0.7 miles, turn right on Bay Road. In 0.9 miles, there is a parking lot for Ravenswood Open Space Preserve.
