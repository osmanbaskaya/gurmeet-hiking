Route
From the parking lot along McKean Road, follow Figueroa Trail (1.4 miles) → left on Canada Del Oro Trail (0.8 miles) → left on Canada Del Oro Cutoff (0.2 miles) → left on Serpentine Loop Trail (2.0 miles) → Longwall Canyon Trail (2.5 miles) → Bald Peaks Trail (1.2 miles) → left on Chisnantuck Peak Trail (1.0 miles) → Cottle Trail (1.3 miles) → Javelina Loop (1.1 miles) → Pena Trail (0.6 miles) → Los Cerritos Trail (0.4 miles) → left on Figueroa Trail (0.2 miles).

Trailhead

Location: on McKean Road in San Jose, CA 95037 . No street address. Parking area is visible in Google Maps if you zoom in sufficiently.

Directions: From Highway 101, take the Bailey Road exit and head west 2.7 miles to McKean Road. Turn left and follow the signs to the trail head for Figueora Trail (see trail map).
